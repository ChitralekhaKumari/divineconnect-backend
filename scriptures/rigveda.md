## Chapter 1: Mandala 1


### Verse 1
**Sanskrit:** अग्निमीळे पुरोहितं यज्ञस्य देवम् ऋत्विजम् ।
**English:** I Laud Agni, the chosen Priest, God, minister of sacrifice...
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.1.1

### Verse 2
**Sanskrit:** अ॒ग्निमी॑ळे पु॒रोहि॑तं य॒ज्ञस्य॑ दे॒वमृ॒त्विज॑म् । होता॑रं रत्न॒धात॑मम् ॥
**English:** I laud Agni, the High Priest, the God, the minister of sacrifice, the Invoker (Hotar), best bestower of treasure.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.1.1

### Verse 3
**Sanskrit:** होता᳚रं रत्न॒धात॑मम् ।
**English:** Who is the Hotar, lavishest of wealth...
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.1.2

### Verse 4
**Sanskrit:** उप॑ त्वाग्ने दि॒वेदि॑वे॒ दोषा॑वस्तर्धि॒या व॒यम् । नमो॒ भर॑न्त॒ एम॑सि ॥
**English:** To thee, Dispenser of the night and day, O Agni, we draw near, with prayer, bearing our homage.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.1.7

### Verse 5
**Sanskrit:** प्राचीनदृष्टिभिः यथा जीवन् अग्निः प्रशंसाय योग्यः अस्ति।
**English:** Worthy is Agni to be praised by living as by ancient seers.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.2.1

### Verse 6
**Sanskrit:** वायवा याहि दर्शतेमे सोमा अरंक्र्ताः |तेषां पाहि शरुधी हवम
**English:** BEAUTIFUL Vāyu, come, for thee these Soma drops have been prepared: Drink of them, hearken to our call.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.2.1

### Verse 7
**Sanskrit:** वा॒यवा या॑हि द॒र्शते॒मे सोमा॒ अरं॑ कृ॒ताः । तेषां॑ पिब शुचिषिप॒रया॒ धिया॑ ॥
**English:** Beautiful Vayu, come: these Soma juices have been prepared. Drink them with pure intention.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.2.1

### Verse 8
**Sanskrit:** वा॒यवा या॑हि द॒र्शते॒मे सोमा॒ अरं॑ कृ॒ताः । तेषां॑ पिब शुचिषिप॒रया॒ धिया॑ ॥
**English:** Beautiful Vayu, come: these Soma drops have been prepared for you. Drink of them, hearken to our call.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.2.1

### Verse 9
**Sanskrit:** वाय उक्थेभिर्जरन्ते तवामछा जरितारः |सुतसोमा अहर्विदः ||
**English:** Knowing the days, with Soma juice poured forth, the singers glorify Thee, Vāyu, with their hymns of praise.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.2.2

### Verse 10
**Sanskrit:** वायो तव परप्र्ञ्चती धेना जिगाति दाशुषे |उरूची सोमपीतये ||
**English:** Vāyu, thy penetrating stream goes forth unto the worshipper,Far-spreading for the Soma draught.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.2.3

### Verse 11
**Sanskrit:** मित्रं हुवे पूतदक्षं वरुणं च रिशादसम | धियं घर्ताचीं साधन्ता ||
**English:** Mitra, of holy strength, I call, and foe-destroying Varuṇa, Who make the oil-fed rite complete.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.2.7

### Verse 12
**Sanskrit:** मि॒त्रं हु॑वे पू॒तद॑क्षं॒ वरु॑णं च रि॒शाद॑सम् । धियं॑ घृ॒ताचीं॒ साध॑न्ता ॥
**English:** I call Mitra, of purified power, and foe-destroying Varuna, completing the rite rich in ghee.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.2.7

### Verse 13
**Sanskrit:** अश्विना यज्वरीरिषो दरवत्पाणी शुभस पती | पुरुभुजाचनस्यतम ||
**English:** YE Aśvins, rich in treasure, Lords of splendour, having nimble hands, Accept the sacrificial food.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.3.1

### Verse 14
**Sanskrit:** अश्विना पुरुदंससा नरा शवीरया धिया | धिष्ण्या वनतं गिरः ||
**English:** Ye Aśvins, rich in wondrous deeds, ye heroes worthy of our praise, Accept our songs with mighty thought.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.3.2

### Verse 15
**Sanskrit:** दस्रा युवाकवः सुता नासत्या वर्क्तबर्हिषः | आ यातंरुद्रवर्तनी ||
**English:** Nāsatyas, wonder-workers, yours are these libations with clipt grass: Come ye whose paths are red with flame.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.3.3

### Verse 16
**Sanskrit:** इन्द्रा याहि चित्रभानो सुता इमे तवायवः |अण्वीभिस्तना पूतासः ||
**English:** O Indra marvellously bright, come, these libations long for thee, Thus by fine fingers purified.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.3.4

### Verse 17
**Sanskrit:** इन्द्रा याहि धियेषितो विप्रजूतः सुतावतः | उप बरह्माणि वाघतः ||
**English:** Urged by the holy singer, sped by song, come, Indra, to the prayers,Of the libation-pouring priest
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.3.5

### Verse 18
**Sanskrit:** आ दे॒वा॑सो॒ ममूहि॑रे॒ विश्वे॑ दे॒वास॒ आदितः॑ । हव्य॒दावा॑नमू॒तये᳚ ॥
**English:** The Gods, all of them, from the beginning, have directed the offering-giver to wealth.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.3.7

### Verse 19
**Sanskrit:** विश्वे देवासो अप्तुरः सुतमा गन्त तूर्णयः | उस्रा इवस्वसराणि ||
**English:** Ye Viśvedevas, swift at work, come hither quickly to the draught, As milch-kine hasten to their stalls.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.3.8

### Verse 20
**Sanskrit:** विश्वे देवासो अस्रिध एहिमायासो अद्रुहः | मेधं जुषन्त वह्नयः ||
**English:** The Viśvedevas, changing shape like serpents, fearless, void of guile, Bearers, accept the sacred draught
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.3.9

### Verse 21
**Sanskrit:** पावका नः सरस्वती वाजेभिर्वाजिनीवती | यज्ञं वष्टु धियावसुः ||
**English:** Wealthy in spoil, enriched with hymns, may bright Sarasvatī desire,With eager love, our sacrifice
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.3.10

### Verse 22
**Sanskrit:** चोदयित्री सून्र्तानां चेतन्ती सुमतीनाम | यज्ञं दधे सरस्वती ||
**English:** Inciter of all pleasant songs, inspirer of all gracious thought, Sarasvatī accept our rite
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.3.11

### Verse 23
**Sanskrit:** महो अर्णः सरस्वती पर चेतयति केतुना | धियो विश्वा वि राजति ||
**English:** Sarasvatī, the mighty flood,—she with her light illuminates, She brightens every pious thought.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.3.12

### Verse 24
**Sanskrit:** सु॒रू॒प॒कृ॒त्नुमू॒तये॑ सु॒दुघा॑मिव गो॒दुहे᳚ । जु॒हू॒मसि॒ द्यवि॑द्यवि ॥
**English:** We call the fair-performing Indra for succor day by day, like the milch-cow to the milker.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.4.1

### Verse 25
**Sanskrit:** सु॒रू॒प॒कृ॒त्नुमू॒तये॑ सु॒दुघा॑मिव गो॒दुहे᳚ । जु॒हू॒मसि॒ द्यवि॑द्यवि ॥
**English:** We call the fair-performing Indra for succor day by day, like the milch-cow to the milker.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.4.1

### Verse 26
**Sanskrit:** सुरूपक्र्त्नुमूतये सुदुघामिव गोदुहे |जुहूमसि दयवि-दयवि ||
**English:** As a good cow to him who milks, we call the doer of fair deeds, To our assistance day by day.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.4.2

### Verse 27
**Sanskrit:** उत बरुवन्तु नो निदो निरन्यतश्चिदारत | दधाना इन्द्र इद दुवः ||
**English:** Whether the men who mock us say, Depart unto another place, Ye who serve Indra and none else;
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.4.5

### Verse 28
**Sanskrit:** उत नः सुभगानरिर्वोचेयुर्दस्म कर्ष्टयः | सयामेदिन्द्रस्य शर्मणि |
**English:** Or whether, God of wondrous deeds, all our true people call us blest, Still may we dwell in Indra's care.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.4.6

### Verse 29
**Sanskrit:** वि बृ॑ह॒ता सु॒शुष्मि॑णा॒ वरा॑य शत्रुम॒र्धिन॑ । इन्द्रा॒भ्यम॑र्दिषत् ॥
**English:** He cleft the cloud, ever bounteous Indra, irresistible hero! to win the great spoil by his great might.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.7.6

### Verse 30
**Sanskrit:** अग्निं दूतं वर्णीमहे होतारं विश्ववेदसम | अस्य यज्ञस्य सुक्रतुम ||
**English:** WE choose Agni the messenger, the herald, master of all wealth, Well skilled in this our sacrifice.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.12.1

### Verse 31
**Sanskrit:** अ॒ग्निं दू॒तं वृ॑णीमहे॒ होता॑रं वि॒श्ववे॑दसम् । अ॒स्य य॒ज्ञस्य॑ सु॒क्रतुम् ॥
**English:** We choose Agni as our messenger, the Invoker who knows all things, the efficient manager of this sacrifice.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.12.1

### Verse 32
**Sanskrit:** स नः पावक दीदिवो.अग्ने देवानिहा वह | उप यज्ञं हविश्च नः ||
**English:** Such, Agni, Purifier, bright, bring hither to our sacrifice,To our oblation bring the Gods.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.12.10

### Verse 33
**Sanskrit:** मि॒त्रावरु॑णा॒वश्वि॑ना॒ त्वाष्ट॒रमव॑ सेधतम् । ब्र॒ह्म॒णा व॑र्धय॒तम् ॥
**English:** Mitra-Varuna, the Ashvins, and Tvashtar, urge on our prayer; promote it with devotion.
**Summary:** Rigveda 1.15.6

### Verse 34
**Sanskrit:** मि॒त्रावरु॑णा॒वश्वि॑ना॒ त्वाष्ट॒रमव॑ सेधतम् । ब्र॒ह्म॒णा व॑र्धय॒तम् ॥
