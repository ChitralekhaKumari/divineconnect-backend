## Sanskrit

ब्रह्ममुरारिसुरार्चितलिङ्गं निर्मलभासितशोभितलिङ्गम्।
जन्मजदुःखविनाशकलिङ्गं तत्प्रणमामि सदाशिवलिङ्गम्॥ १॥

देवमुनिप्रवरार्चितलिङ्गं कामदहनकरुणाकरलिङ्गम्।
रावणदर्पविनाशनलिङ्गं तत्प्रणमामि सदाशिवलिङ्गम्॥ २॥

सर्वसुगन्धिसुलेपितलिङ्गं बुद्धिविवर्धनकारणलिङ्गम्।
सिद्धसुरासुरवन्दितलिङ्गं तत्प्रणमामि सदाशिवलिङ्गम्॥ ३॥

कनकमहामणिभूषितलिङ्गं फणिपतिवेष्टितशोभितलिङ्गम्।
दक्षसुयज्ञविनाशनलिङ्गं तत्प्रणमामि सदाशिवलिङ्गम्॥ ४॥

कुङ्कुमचन्दनलेपितलिङ्गं पङ्कजहारसुशोभितलिङ्गम्।
सञ्चितपापविनाशनलिङ्गं तत्प्रणमामि सदाशिवलिङ्गम्॥ ५॥

देवगणार्चितसेवितलिङ्गं भावैर्भक्तिभिरेव च लिङ्गम्।
दिनकरकोटिप्रभाकरलिङ्गं तत्प्रणमामि सदाशिवलिङ्गम्॥ ६॥

अष्टदलोपरिवेष्टितलिङ्गं सर्वसमुद्भवकारणलिङ्गम्।
अष्टदरिद्रविनाशनलिङ्गं तत्प्रणमामि सदाशिवलिङ्गम्॥ ७॥

सुरगुरुसुरवरपूजितलिङ्गं सुरवनपुष्पसदार्चितलिङ्गम्।
परात्परं परमात्मकलिङ्गं तत्प्रणमामि सदाशिवलिङ्गम्॥ ८॥

लिङ्गाष्टकमिदं पुण्यं यः पठेत् शिवसन्निधौ।
शिवलोकमवाप्नोति शिवेन सह मोदते॥

इति श्रीशिवलिङ्गाष्टकं सम्पूर्णम्॥

## Transliteration

Brahma Murari Surarchita Lingam Nirmala Bhasita Shobhita Lingam, Janmaja Duhkha Vinashaka Lingam Tat Pranamami Sadashiva Lingam. (1)

Devamuni Pravararchita Lingam Kamadahana Karunakara Lingam, Ravana Darpa Vinashana Lingam Tat Pranamami Sadashiva Lingam. (2)

Sarva Sugandhi Sulepita Lingam Buddhi Vivardhana Karana Lingam, Siddha Surasura Vandita Lingam Tat Pranamami Sadashiva Lingam. (3)

Kanaka Mahamani Bhushita Lingam Phanipati Veshtita Shobhita Lingam, Daksha Suyajna Vinashana Lingam Tat Pranamami Sadashiva Lingam. (4)

Kunkuma Chandana Lepita Lingam Pankaja Hara Sushobhita Lingam, Sanchita Papa Vinashana Lingam Tat Pranamami Sadashiva Lingam. (5)

Devaganarchita Sevita Lingam Bhavair Bhaktibhireva Cha Lingam, Dinakara Koti Prabhakara Lingam Tat Pranamami Sadashiva Lingam. (6)

Ashtadalopari Veshtita Lingam Sarvasamudbhava Karana Lingam, Ashtadaridra Vinashana Lingam Tat Pranamami Sadashiva Lingam. (7)

Suraguru Suravara Pujita Lingam Suravana Pushpa Sadarchita Lingam, Paratparam Paramatmaka Lingam Tat Pranamami Sadashiva Lingam. (8)

Lingashtakamidam Punyam Yah Pathet Shivasannidhau, Shivalokamavapnoti Shivena Saha Modate.

Iti Shri Shiva Lingashtakam Sampurnam.

## Meaning

An eight-verse hymn glorifying the Shivalinga, the abstract symbolic form of Lord Shiva. Each verse praises a different quality of the Linga — worshipped by Brahma, Vishnu and the gods (1); revered by sages, having burnt away desire and destroyed the pride of Ravana (2); anointed with fragrances and increasing wisdom, worshipped by siddhas, devas and asuras (3); adorned with gold and gems, encircled by the serpent-king, and connected to the destruction of Daksha's arrogant sacrifice (4); smeared with kumkum and sandal paste, garlanded with lotuses, destroying accumulated sins (5); served by hosts of gods with devotion, shining like a million suns (6); surrounded by an eight-petalled base, the cause of all manifestation and destroyer of the eight forms of poverty (7); and finally worshipped by the preceptor and the best of the gods with flowers from the celestial garden, supreme beyond the supreme (8). The closing verse promises that whoever recites this Ashtakam in Shiva's presence attains his abode and rejoices with him.

## Benefits

Recited during Shiva puja, Rudrabhishekam, and especially on Mondays and Maha Shivaratri. The text's own closing verse states that reciting it with devotion near a Shivalinga leads to attaining Shivaloka (Shiva's abode) and eternal bliss in his company. Devotees chant it to remove the sorrows born of the cycle of birth and death, increase wisdom, destroy accumulated sins, and overcome poverty and adversity — invoking Shiva's grace through his most sacred symbolic form.
