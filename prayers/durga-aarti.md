## Sanskrit

जय अम्बे गौरी, मैया जय श्यामा गौरी। तुमको निशदिन ध्यावत, हरि ब्रह्मा शिवरी॥ माँग सिंदूर विराजत, टीको मृगमद को। उज्ज्वल से दोउ नैना, चंद्रवदन नीको॥ कनक समान कलेवर, रक्ताम्बर राजै। रक्तपुष्प गल माला, कंठन पर साजै॥ केहरि वाहन राजत, खड्ग खप्परधारी। सुर-नर मुनिजन सेवत, तिनके दुखहारी॥ कानन कुण्डल शोभित, नासाग्रे मोती। कोटिक चंद्र दिवाकर, राजत सम ज्योती॥ शुम्भ निशुम्भ विदारे, महिषासुर घाती। धूम्र विलोचन नैना, निशदिन मदमाती॥ चण्ड-मुण्ड संहारे, शोणित बीज हरे। मधु-कैटभ दोउ मारे, सुर भयहीन करे॥ ब्रह्माणी रुद्राणी तुम कमला रानी। आगम निगम बखानी, तुम शिव पटरानी॥ चौंसठ योगिनी मंगल गावत, नृत्य करत भैरों। बाजत ताल मृदंगा, अरु बाजत डमरू॥ तुम ही जग की माता, तुम ही हो भरता। भक्तन की दुख हरता, सुख सम्पत्ति करता॥ भुजा चार अति शोभित, खड्ग खप्परधारी। मनवांछित फल पावत, सेवत नर नारी॥ कंचन थाल विराजत, अगर कपूर बाती। श्रीमालकेतु में राजत, कोटि रतन ज्योती॥ श्री अम्बेजी की आरती जो कोई नर गावै। कहत शिवानंद स्वामी, सुख सम्पत्ति पावै॥

## Meaning

Victory to you, O bright Amba, O dark Shyama Gauri — Vishnu, Brahma and Shiva meditate upon you day and night. Your forehead is adorned with sindoor and musk tilak, your two eyes are radiant and your moonlike face is beautiful. Your form is golden, robed in red, and a garland of red flowers adorns your neck. You ride the lion, bearing sword and skull-cup, and you remove the sorrows of gods, men and sages who serve you. Earrings adorn your ears and a pearl gleams at your nose, your radiance equals that of millions of suns and moons. You slew Shumbha, Nishumbha and Mahishasura, and your eyes are eternally intoxicated with divine power. You destroyed Chanda and Munda, vanquished Raktabija and slew Madhu and Kaitabha, freeing the gods from fear. You are Brahmani, Rudrani and Kamala the queen, praised in the Agamas and Nigamas, the beloved consort of Shiva. Sixty-four Yoginis sing auspicious songs, Bhairava dances and the beats of tabla, mridanga and damru resound. You are the mother of the universe and its sustainer, remover of devotees sorrows and bestower of happiness and wealth. Your four arms are resplendent bearing sword and skull-cup, and men and women who serve you attain their hearts desires. A golden plate shines with agarbatti and camphor flame, and in Shri Malaket countless jewels glow with divine light. Shivanand Swami declares that whoever sings this aarti of Shri Ambe shall attain happiness and prosperity.

## Benefits

The complete evening aarti of Goddess Durga, as composed by Shivanand Swami, sung in homes and temples especially during Navaratri. It praises the Goddess in her many forms — Gauri, Shyama, Brahmani, Rudrani and Kamala — and recounts her victories over demons such as Mahishasura, Shumbha, Nishumbha and Raktabija. Singing this aarti with devotion is believed to grant protection, fulfilment of desires, and happiness and prosperity.
